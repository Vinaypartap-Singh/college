Pulse Candy: How Sweet Is This!

Introduction

Pulse Candy is a tangy and spicy candy that has taken the Indian market by storm. Known for its unique blend of flavors, it offers an unforgettable taste experience.

Interesting Facts

  • Pulse Candy was launched in 2015 and quickly became a sensation.
  • It comes in various flavors, including Kachcha Aam (raw mango), Guava, and Lemon.
  • The candy's tangy core is what sets it apart from other sweets.
